M A R I S S A R E I L L Y

As noted above, my name is Marissa. I am from Portsmouth, NH, a lovely and quaint towm along a portion of New Hampshire's 18 miles of seacoast. Following adolescence, I headed off to Poughkeepsie, NY to attend Vassar College where I played lacrosse, ran the hip hop club and explored the likes of Matthew Barney. It was a quintessential liberal arts experience. Following graduation, I moved to Oakland, CA to pursue dreams of planning. I eventually got a job at the planning and design firm, MIG where I was a project associate for four years. Outside of my job, I spent most time biking up and down chaparral hills and gallivanting under the California sun. And now I go to MIT.
